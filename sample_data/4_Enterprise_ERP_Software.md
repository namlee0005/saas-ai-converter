# NexusCloud ERP - Enterprise Solutions

## 1. Product Overview
**NexusCloud ERP** is an AI-powered, cloud-native Enterprise Resource Planning suite designed for mid-to-large manufacturing and logistics companies. Modules include Financials, Supply Chain Management (SCM), Human Capital Management (HCM), and AI Predictive Analytics.

## 2. Pricing Tiers (SaaS Subscription)
- **Growth Tier:** $5,000/month. Up to 50 users. Core modules (Finance, Basic HR, Inventory). 99.9% Uptime SLA.
- **Enterprise Tier:** $15,000/month. Up to 500 users. Advanced modules (AI Forecasting, Global Payroll, Advanced SCM). Dedicated Success Manager. 99.99% Uptime SLA.
- **On-Premise / Private Cloud:** Starts at $250,000/year license + $50,000 Implementation fee.

- **Phase 1 (Month 1-2):** Discovery & Data Mapping.

## 4. Service Level Agreement (SLA) & Penalties
- **Uptime:** If uptime falls below 99.99% (approx 4.38 minutes downtime/month), we issue a 10% credit for that month's billing. Below 99.0% yields a 50% credit.
- **Data Security:** We are SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, GDPR, and HIPAA compliant. Data is encrypted AES-256 at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit.
