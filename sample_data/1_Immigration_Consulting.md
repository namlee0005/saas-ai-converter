# Maple Leaf Visa Solutions - Comprehensive Knowledge Base

## 1. Company Overview & Core Values
**Maple Leaf Visa Solutions (MLVS)** is a premier Canadian immigration and education consulting firm established in 2010, headquartered in Toronto, Ontario with branch offices in Vancouver, Vietnam, and India. We are registered with the College of Immigration and Citizenship Consultants (CICC).
**Mission:** To provide transparent, ethical, and high-success-rate pathways for global citizens to study, work, and live in Canada.

## 2. Detailed Services & Fee Structure

### 2.1 Express Entry (Federal Skilled Worker)
- **Target Audience:** Highly skilled professionals (IT, Healthcare, Finance).
- **Requirements:** IELTS 7.0+ (minimum 6.0 in each band), Bachelor's or Master's degree (ECA assessed), minimum 3 years of continuous work experience.
- **Processing Time:** 6 - 8 months from profile submission.
- **Consulting Fee:** $3,000 USD.
- **Government Fees (not included):** $850 processing fee + $515 right of permanent residence fee.

### 2.2 Provincial Nominee Program (PNP) - Ontario & BC
- **Target Audience:** Tech workers, international students graduating from Canadian institutions.
- **Processing Time:** 12 - 18 months.
- **Consulting Fee:** $4,500 USD (Includes Express Entry profile + Provincial nomination application).

### 2.3 Startup Visa (SUV)
- **Target Audience:** Entrepreneurs with innovative tech ideas.
- **Requirements:** Must secure a Letter of Support from a designated Angel Investor, Venture Capital, or Business Incubator. Minimum IELTS 5.0. No strict age limit.
- **Processing Time:** 12 - 36 months for Permanent Residency. Work permit available in 4 months.
- **Consulting Fee:** $10,000 USD (Legal & Consulting only). Incubator training fees (approx. $15,000 - $30,000) are separate and paid directly to the incubator.

### 2.4 Study Permits & Student Visas
- **Target Audience:** High school and university students.
- **Consulting Fee:** $1,500 USD. (Free if applying to our partnered institutions).
- **Financial Proof Required:** At least $20,635 CAD for living expenses + 1 year of tuition.

## 3. Application Process & Workflow
1. **Initial Assessment:** Free profile evaluation via AI Chatbot.
2. **Consultation:** 1-on-1 session with a CICC licensed consultant ($150 fee, refundable if contract signed).
3. **Contract Signing:** 50% retainer fee paid.
4. **Document Preparation:** Translation, notarization, ECA, and IELTS prep.
5. **Submission & Tracking:** Submission to IRCC. Remaining 50% fee due upon ITA (Invitation to Apply).
6. **Post-Landing Services:** Housing, SIN registration, and bank account setup (Optional add-on: $500).

## 4. Refund & Cancellation Policy
- **Express Entry:** If the client's profile is not drawn within 12 months, 30% of the consulting fee is refunded.
- **Visa Denial:** If the visa is denied due to an error on our part, 100% of the consulting fee is refunded. If denied due to client misrepresentation (fraudulent documents, hidden criminal record), NO REFUND.

## 5. Frequently Asked Questions (Deep Dive)
- **Can I bring my spouse and children?** Yes. Spouses can get an Open Work Permit (OWP). Children can attend Canadian public schools for free.
- **What happens if my IELTS expires?** IELTS is valid for 2 years. If it expires in the pool, you must retake it and update your profile immediately.
- **Do you guarantee PR?** By Canadian law, no consultant can guarantee approval. We guarantee 100% legal compliance and maximum points optimization.
- **How do I book a consultation?** Please provide your name, phone number, and email. Our AI Assistant will instantly book a slot on our Consultant's calendar.

## 6. Complex Edge Cases & Legal Disclaimers
- **Medical Inadmissibility:** If a client or their dependent has a medical condition that could place an "excessive demand" on Canadian health/social services (e.g., severe autism, chronic renal failure requiring dialysis - exceeding $26,270 CAD/year), the application may be denied. MLVS charges an additional $5,000 USD for a Mitigation Plan involving medical legal experts to fight procedural fairness letters.
- **Criminal Inadmissibility (DUI):** A past DUI (Driving Under Influence) conviction makes you criminally inadmissible to Canada. You must apply for Criminal Rehabilitation. Wait time: 5 years from the end of the sentence. Fee: $2,500 USD.
- **Provincial Nominee Program (PNP) Defaulting:** If you receive PR through a PNP (e.g., Saskatchewan) but immediately move to Ontario upon landing, the province can flag you for misrepresentation and IRCC can strip your PR status. MLVS mandates clients sign an "Intent to Reside" compliance form.
