Simple and secure to earn up 8.50% Aptos staking rewards

**What is AMNIS?**

Amnis Finance - a **Pioneering Liquidity Staking on Aptos**. As a foundational component of the Aptos ecosystem, Amnis Finance introduces a secure, user-friendly and innovative liquid staking protocol that empowers users to effortlessly maximize returns on their APT tokens while unlocking their liquidity.

**Features**

1.   **Liquid Staking on Aptos** 

Traditional Proof-of-Stake ̣̣̣̣̣(PoS) protocols used to be about users locking their tokens and receiving an anticipated amount of staking reward in exchange. However, because these tokens are locked, users are prevented from trading or investing in other token pairs on other platforms. This consequently restricts the ability to explore higher-yield opportunities.

Amnis Finance offers the capability of liquid staking, a pivotal concept in the realm of cryptocurrencies and blockchain technology. Through this feature, Amnis allows users to instantly receive tokens of equivalent value to those they have staked, enabling them to make use of the value of these tokens in transactions or other activities of interest, all without necessitating the unstaking process and the subsequent waiting period for token release. Liquid staking also provides flexibility for users to take advantage of trading or investment opportunities without having to withdraw tokens from the PoS network.

1.   **Yield tokenization** 

Amnis aims to create a new financial instrument that allows for more flexibility in crafting payoff strategies and opens gateways for institutions to enter the market and enabling traders to leverage future yields.

First, Amnis wraps yield-bearing assets (stAPT) into SY (standardized yield tokens). SY is then split into its principal and yield components, PT (principal token) and YT (yield token) respectively, for maximum control. This process is termed as yield-tokenization, where the yield is tokenized into a separate token. PT represents the original value of the staked asset, At maturity, PT can get back to the original asset at the ratio of 1:1. Additionally, YT illustrates the portion of the interest that will be generated. Holding YT allows the users to receive the entire yield, but no principal repayment at the end.

**Comparison with other Aptos staking options:**

Amnis Finance offers a staking APR of 8.5%, which is higher than that of other protocols. In addition to this comparison, Amnis Finance provides APT Derivative tokens, including stAPT and amAPT, whereas other only offers stAPT or tAPT. Notably, Amnis Finance secures the backing of the Aptos Foundation, offering token utilities such as Governance and Incentives, as well as Institutional KYC Support and an Affiliate system. Unfortunately, specific information regarding these features is not available for the other alternatives.

Amnis Finance remains committed to being a user-friendly protocol, with amAPT maintaining a stable 1:1 peg to APT. In contrast, tokens from other projects experience fluctuations in value over time. Additionally, Amnis Finance prioritizes user convenience by offering swift withdrawals with zero fees, while other platforms may incur fees and lack similar support mechanisms.

## ⛏️How Amnis works?

**Requirements:**

*   Wallet that supports Aptos: 

Stake APT[](https://docs.amnis.finance/amnis-protocol/how-amnis-works#stake-apt)

**Step 1: Acquire APT**

Staking APT via Amnis requires you to have APT. For example, you may purchase APT on centralized exchanges like [Coinbase](https://www.coinbase.com/) or [Binance](https://binance.com/) from fiat currency.

Once purchased, withdraw the APT to your Aptos-supported wallet. ([Coinbase](https://help.coinbase.com/en/coinbase/trading-and-funding/cryptocurrency-trading-pairs/how-to-send-and-receive-cryptocurrency), [Binance](https://www.binance.com/en/support/faq/115003670492))

**Step 2: Connect wallet to Amnis Finance**

Now that you have APT in your Aptos-supported wallet, navigate to the [Amnis Finance app](https://testnet.amnis.finance/mint).

On the [Stake](https://stake.amnis.finance/mint)tab, click "Connect Wallet".

Select the wallet that you have installed and approve the app.

![Image 2](https://docs.amnis.finance/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F135941930-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FS1BuIoYSwiJZqNrZeQzd%252Fuploads%252Fv5Awq0Wso0gMftrnc4EP%252Fimage.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Dc7004c97-3eba-4411-bf08-30c52ea6e3b9&width=300&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=9331bc7f&sv=2)

**Step 3: Stake your APT**![Image 3](https://docs.amnis.finance/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F135941930-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FS1BuIoYSwiJZqNrZeQzd%252Fuploads%252FQma9h8wj3UAfZJKxdlWq%252Fimage.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Df0ce1c4d-f040-426a-bc18-c7b6c2878bca&width=300&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=e2e1ff34&sv=2)

Simply by holding [stAPT](https://docs.amnis.finance/amnis-protocol/amapt-and-stapt)in your wallet, you can earn passive income at an approximate +7.5% APR (Annual Percentage Rate). Additionally, you can use [stAPT](https://docs.amnis.finance/amnis-protocol/amapt-and-stapt) for lending on [Aries](https://app.ariesmarkets.xyz/lending), staking, and yield farming within the Aptos DeFI ecosystem.

# 🪙amAPT and stAPT

**amAPT**

![Image 5](https://docs.amnis.finance/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F135941930-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FS1BuIoYSwiJZqNrZeQzd%252Fuploads%252FpESwHPOCsoiMHwSkSCsJ%252FamAPT.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D1afcdf8d-6d44-4135-9bbd-15ad3f248600&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=b04d74f0&sv=2)

amAPT logo

amAPT (Amnis Aptos coin) acts as a stablecoin loosely pegged to APT, so that 1 amAPT always represents 1 APT and the amount of amAPT in circulation matches the amount of APT in the Amnis Finance. When APT is sent to the Amnis Finance, an equivalent amount of amAPT is minted.

1.     Get amAPT: [Mint amtAPT](https://stake.amnis.finance/mint) directly from APT on Amnis Finance. 
2.     Uses of amAPT in Aptos DeFI: 

*     [Stake amAPT](https://stake.amnis.finance/stake) to stAPT to get 8,5% APR (the APR will be changed due to the amount of amAPT held by stakers) 
*     Add liquidity on [Cellana](https://app.cellana.finance/swap?inputCurrency=0x1%3A%3Aaptos_coin%3A%3AAptosCoin&outputCurrency=0x111ae3e5bc816a5e63c2da97d0aa3886519e0cd5e4b046659fa35796bd11542a%3A%3Aamapt_token%3A%3AAmnisApt), [Pancakeswap](https://aptos.pancakeswap.finance/add/0x111ae3e5bc816a5e63c2da97d0aa3886519e0cd5e4b046659fa35796bd11542a::amapt_token::AmnisApt/0x1::aptos_coin::AptosCoin), [Liquidswap](https://liquidswap.com/#/?from=0x1::aptos_coin::AptosCoin&to=0x111ae3e5bc816a5e63c2da97d0aa3886519e0cd5e4b046659fa35796bd11542a::amapt_token::AmnisApt&curve=stable&version=0.5&target=swap). 
*     Lending on [Aries](https://app.ariesmarkets.xyz/lending), [Meso](https://app.meso.finance/markets), [Echelon](https://app.echelon.market/dashboard). 
*     Staking and yield farming in Aptos DeFI ecosystem. 

#### [](https://docs.amnis.finance/amnis-protocol/amapt-and-stapt#amapt-apt-liquidity-pool)

amAPT/APT liquidity pool

The amAPT/APT liquidity pool provides users with a fast and convenient way to withdraw amAPT instantly, bypassing the standard 14-day epoch wait time. With a 1:1 peg between amAPT and APT and deep liquidity across multiple DEXs, users can easily swap between the two tokens.

To address situations where market demand for amAPT or APT surges significantly, Amnis has established a reserve mechanism. When necessary, the protocol will buy back amAPT from the market to stabilize its price. Any profit generated from this mechanism will be transferred directly to the Amnis treasury. Additionally, Amnis has developed a robust system equipped with monitoring alerts and AI-powered algorithms to ensure the peg remains stable and effectively managed.

# 💫Yield tokenization

Amnis wraps yield-bearing assets (stAPT) into SY (standardized yield tokens). SY is then split into its principal and yield components, PT (principal token) and YT (yield token) respectively, for maximum control. This process is termed as yield-tokenization, where the yield is tokenized into a separate token.

![Image 5](https://docs.amnis.finance/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F135941930-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FS1BuIoYSwiJZqNrZeQzd%252Fuploads%252FrUu2OzVS7Xu7lT4PTQdS%252FAmnis%2520Finance%2520Pitchdeck%2520Main.jpg%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D957f876c-9e57-4e3e-8b0c-03dd90fec64f&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=736af8d2&sv=2)

**Principal Tokens (PT)**

**PT**represents the original value of the staked asset, At maturity, PT can get back to the original asset at the ratio of 1:1.

**Yield Token (YT)**

**YT**illustrates the portion of the interest that will be generated. Holding YT allows the users to receive the entire yield, but no principal repayment at the end.

**Fixed APY**

Fixed APY is the guaranteed yield you will receive by holding PT. This value is calculated based on the ratio of the price of YT to PT and the formula is shown below.

# 🥂Fees

The current protocol fee on the mainnet is 7%.

Amnis Finance applies a 7% fee on staking rewards to contribute to the DAO Treasury. This equates to approximately 0.49% of the deposited APT's value per year, or $0.49 for every $100 in deposits.

This fee level should make Amnis staking more competitive than what is offered with most exchange staking alternatives.

The fee is subject to change by the DAO through a successful vote.

# 🏛️Token Utilities

Amnis token (AMI)

AMI is the governance token of Amnis, a DAO operating on the Aptos blockchain. Amnis is the protocol that created [amAPT](https://docs.amnis.finance/amnis-protocol/amapt-and-stapt) and [stAPT](https://docs.amnis.finance/amnis-protocol/amapt-and-stapt), liquid staking tokens representing staked APT in the Amnis stake pool.

TOKEN FEATURES

**DAO GOVERNANCE**

*     AMI is the governance token of Amnis DAO which allows the community to propose, vote and implement changes to on-chain matters relating to the development of the protocol. 

**INCENTIVE**

*     AMI tokens will also be used as incentive to bootstrap the adoption of Amnis LSD staking ecosystem

# 📎How to Join

Our mission is to build a foundational pillar in the Aptos Ecosystem to drive credit expansion through APT staking. We would love to work with partners who run validators with excellent performance and high reliability to benefit users, partners and Aptos ecosystem:

1.     Please, reach out to our head of partnerships: [@hoaha47](https://t.me/hoaha47) on Telegram or email: partnership@amnis.finance to initiate collaboration opportunities.

3.     After the MOU is signed, we will proceed to whitelist addresses of your validator nodes onto Amnis Protocol. 
4.     Additionally, please coordinate with our head of marketing: [@Ajsmith25](https://t.me/Ajsthm25) on Telegram or email: marketing@amnis.finance to explore opportunities for marketing collaboration, ensuring a seamless integration process.
