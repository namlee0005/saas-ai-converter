# Diamond Bay Luxury Villas - Project Factsheet

## 1. Project Master Overview
**Diamond Bay Luxury Villas** is a $500M ultra-luxury beachfront development developed by Apex Holdings. Spanning 50 hectares of pristine coastline, it features 120 limited-edition villas, an 18-hole championship golf course designed by Greg Norman, a 200-berth private marina, and a 5-star clubhouse managed by Ritz-Carlton.
- **Location:** Ocean Drive, Pearl Peninsula. 15 mins from International Airport.
- **Legal Status:** Freehold for Locals; 50-year renewable leasehold for Foreigners.
- **Handover Date:** Q4 2026.

## 2. Villa Types & Detailed Pricing

### 2.1 Ocean-Breeze 3-Bedroom Villa
- **Price:** $1,500,000 USD.
- **Specifications:** 300 sqm land area, 250 sqm build area. 2 floors.
- **Features:** Private 10m infinity pool, lush tropical garden, 2-car garage. Smart home system (Control4).
- **Handover Condition:** Fully furnished (Turnkey) with Italian imported furniture (Minotti, Poliform).

### 2.2 Signature 5-Bedroom Beachfront Estate
- **Price:** $3,000,000 USD to $3,500,000 USD.
- **Specifications:** 600 sqm land area, 500 sqm build area. Direct beach access.
- **Features:** 15m lap pool, private cinema room, wine cellar, separate maid's quarters.
- **Handover Condition:** Bare-shell option available for owners wishing to custom-design interiors (Subtract $300,000 from base price).

## 3. Investment & Rental Yield Program (Guaranteed ROI)
Owners can opt into the **Rental Pool Program** managed by Ritz-Carlton.
- **Guarantee:** 8% net return per annum guaranteed for the first 3 years.
- **Revenue Share:** After year 3, revenue is split 70% (Owner) / 30% (Management).
- **Owner Privileges:** 30 nights of free stay per year (cannot be booked during Peak Season: Dec 20 - Jan 10).

## 4. Payment Schedule (Standard Route)
1. **Reservation (Booking):** $20,000 USD (Non-refundable, but transferable to another unit).
2. **1st Installment (Sign SPA):** 20% (minus booking fee) within 14 days.
3. **2nd to 5th Installments:** 10% every 6 months during construction.
4. **Handover:** 35% upon receiving the keys (Q4 2026).
5. **Title Deed:** 5% upon issuance of the pink book/title deed.

## 5. Frequently Asked Questions
- **Can foreigners buy this property?** Yes. Foreigners can purchase under a 50-year lease agreement, which can be extended. They can also participate in the Rental Program.
- **What are the monthly maintenance fees?** $2.50 USD per square meter of land area. This covers security, landscaping, pool cleaning, and clubhouse access.
- **Is financing available?** Yes, partnered with Standard Chartered Bank for up to 60% LTV for locals. Foreigners must pay 100% cash.
- **How do I arrange a site visit?** We offer a complimentary VIP helicopter or limousine pickup from the airport. Leave your email and phone number, and our AI will book a VIP Tour with our Senior Broker.
